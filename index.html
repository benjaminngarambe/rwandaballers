The Vision

Talent is Universal.
Opportunity is Not.

Rwandaballers exists to close the gap between raw potential in Africa and the global professional standards. We don't just teach drills; we teach Basketball IQ, professional habits, and life pathways.

Our goal is to prove that young African athletes don't have to choose between a career and a sport. They can dominate in both.

The Journey Behind the Project

"Founded by Benjamin Ngarambe, his basketball journey started in Kigali, Rwanda at 14. By 18, he moved to Poland to study Computer Science, dreaming of balancing his professional basketball dreams with academics. He underestimated the challenge."

Between the language barrier, demanding studies, and the need to work to survive, he almost quit. But in 2020, With the appropriate support he locked in. He was spotted at a local park by a pro player, which led to tryouts and eventually a spot in the 2nd Professional League.

"But here is the reality: Even as a pro in Europe, He had to relearn the basics. Passing angles, screen timing, weak-side rotations—things he missed growing up. He had to catch up on Basketball IQ while learning how to recover my body and communicate in a foreign language."

Today, he has graduated with Honors in Computer Science and work full-time as a Technology Consultant while playing professional basketball. He founded Rwandaballers to give early the next generation the right skills & tools he had to learn late the hard way—so they can succeed in basketball, in business, and in life.

Benjamin Ngarambe Founder • Pro Athlete • Tech Consultant
